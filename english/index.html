About Me

Hi! I’m Camila Cala, a Data Analyst and Industrial Engineer who believes that every number tells a story — and every story can inspire better decisions.

My professional path began in the industrial sector, surrounded by the rhythm of production lines and continuous improvement challenges. From ceramic factories to technology companies, from BMW import operations to real estate construction, I’ve had the chance to explore how different industries operate — and what truly makes them efficient, optimizing processes, improving workflows and coordinating teams to bring ambitious projects to life. I loved building structure and efficiency… until life took me on a different kind of project: living abroad.

While traveling and working across Australia and other countries in Asia, I discovered a new way to do what I’ve always loved — improving how things work, but this time through the lens of data. Seeing how technology and analytics were transforming industries sparked a passion that completely reshaped my path.

Today, I bring together the best of both worlds: the process-driven mindset of an engineer and the curiosity of a data analyst. I use tools like Python, SQL, Power BI, and Lean Six Sigma to uncover insights that help teams work smarter, not harder — turning information into strategies that truly make an impact.

Curious mind, structured thinker, and traveler at heart — I’m on a mission to help businesses grow through data, creativity, and a touch of engineering precision.
