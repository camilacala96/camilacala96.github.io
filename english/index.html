About Me

I’m Camilo Díaz, a Data Analyst and Civil Engineer passionate about uncovering insights that drive smarter decisions. My professional path began in the construction industry, where I spent several years coordinating complex projects, optimizing resources, and managing multidisciplinary teams.

During my journey living and working across different countries, I discovered a growing interest in technology and the world of data. Traveling opened my perspective to global innovation and the power of information to transform industries.

Through my Data Analytics program at TripleTen, I gained hands-on experience with Python, SQL, Tableau, Power BI, Looker Studio, and Advanced Excel, developing real-world projects focused on hypothesis testing, A/B experimentation, and data visualization.

Today, I combine my analytical skills with my engineering mindset to deliver insights that are actionable, evidence-based, and aligned with business goals. My goal is to keep growing in the data field and contribute to projects that create measurable impact.
