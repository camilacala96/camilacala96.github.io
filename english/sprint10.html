Analysis of Trending YouTube Videos

Designed an interactive Tableau dashboard to explore YouTube video trends across countries and categories. The visualization combines area charts, a pie chart, and a dynamic summary table to highlight popularity patterns and engagement distribution by percentage and total video count.

Conclusion

The dashboard reveals that Entertainment, Comedy, and Music are the dominant trending categories across all regions, with the United States leading in overall video share, followed closely by France, Russia, and India. Trends remain stable throughout the month, peaking around mid-period, reflecting consistent audience engagement and minimal shifts in viewing preferences. Overall, Western countries show slightly higher representation in global trending content compared to others.
